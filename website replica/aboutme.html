About G.P Nainital

Government Polytechnic Nainital was established in 1957, making it one of the oldest technical institutes in Uttarakhand. G.P. Nainital is widely recognized as one of the best institutions for technical education in the state. The college offers a variety of diploma programs catering to multiple engineering and technical fields. Its curriculum is thoughtfully designed to bridge the gap between theoretical academics and hands-on industry skills. The institute emphasizes both classroom learning and practical training to prepare students for real-world challenges. G.P. Nainital boasts a strong faculty team comprising experienced educators and industry professionals. Industry experts frequently collaborate with the college to provide students with up-to-date knowledge and training. The college maintains a vast and active alumni network spread across top industries in India and abroad. Students benefit from mentorship, internships, and placement support through this network. Government Polytechnic Nainital continues to empower future engineers and technocrats with quality technical education.
Government Polytechnic Nainital (known as Nainital Polytechnic) is a technical education institute located at Nainital, Uttarakhand. Established in 1975, the institute is affiliated from Uttarakhand Technical Education Board, Roorkee and All India Council For Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. Courses offered by institute are Civil Engineering, Computer Application (PGDCA), Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Information Tech., Mechanical Engineering, Modern Office Mgt.& Sec. Prac., Pharmacy. Before Uttarakhand Technical Education Board, Roorkee affiliation Institute was affiliated from UP Technical Board, Lucknow.




GENESIS

Government Polytechnic Nainital is a leading polytechnical institute offering Diploma programmes in several streams of Engineering and Pharmacy. This is the best polytechnical institute of Uttarakhand which was established in the year 1975. A campus with over 1000 students, coached by highly distinguished faculty using the latest teaching aids, excellent hostel facilities, and a vision that drives us to ensure imparting wholesome technical education to our students, is what sets us apart from the run-of-the-mill engineering institutes spread all over the country. An educational oasis located in the pristine Doon valley, the 20 acre GPN campus provides unparalled opportunity for students. A wealth of resources and support services help students get the most out of their time at GPN. From first rate libraries to innovative computer technology, students have access to many tools to explore academic interests. The campus offers diverse resources for the mind, body, and spirit and enough flexibility for students to explore them in their own distinctive ways.
